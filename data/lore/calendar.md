# The Calendar

Source: [d6gaming.org/Campaign_tracker](https://d6gaming.org/index.php/Campaign_tracker)

## Structure

13 months × 4 weeks × 7 days = **364 days per year**.

Year format: **Year-354** (default current year).

Date format: Day-name, Week-name (day-of-month) Month-name — Year-NNN

Example: `Liandra, DeIelina (28) Juba — Year-354`

## Holy Days

One holy day per deity per year — see Mythology for the full list.

## Religious Bonuses by Day

When a follower of a deity is operating on that deity's day or holy day:

- **Lay members** — no benefit
- **Initiates** — +3 on the deity's holy day
- **Priests** — +3 within five days before/after the holy day; **+6** on the day itself

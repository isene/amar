# The World

Sources: [d6gaming.org/The_World](https://d6gaming.org/index.php/The_World), [The Kingdom of Amar](https://d6gaming.org/index.php/The_Kingdom_of_Amar)

## Geography

The world is largely confined by natural barriers:

- **Mountains** dominate the north (Dwarf territory)
- **Forests** cover the west (Araxi territory)
- **Fog** obscures the southern and eastern shores

These barriers limit inhabitants' geographic knowledge beyond the kingdom. The wiki states citizens "have limited geographic awareness beyond the kingdom."

## Climate

Planetary axis tilt is **15°** (compared to Earth's 23.4°), so seasonal variation is mild. The Kingdom of Amar experiences weather **comparable to France**.

## Surrounding Peoples

### The Dwarves

Descendants of cursed human warriors. Sturdy and skilled metalworkers.

- Worship **Barg** and **Alesia**
- Intense fear of heights
- Excellent mining and metal-working
- Hard bargainers; never give anything free
- Skeptical of human magic
- Hatred for trolls and Araxi

**Leadership**: Dwarven King **Gorm** rules from **Borgheim**.

### Trolls

Huge, powerful, solitary creatures thriving on hate.

- Five Grand Chieftains rule territories
- Terrorize surrounding Araxi populations
- Extremely dangerous

### Araxi

Evil forest-dwellers organized in packs.

- Bent body, ~150 cm tall, 130-150 lbs
- Blackened skin, black hair, red eyes, spiked teeth, black claws
- Created from humans via curse — wiki notes "weird effects" including extra-long or short limbs, exceptionally strong or weak individuals, occasional limited magical powers
- Hate everything indiscriminately
- Frequently terrorized by trolls

### Faeries

Otherworldly beings from a purer realm created by Anashina.

**Types**: Elves, Brownies, Pixies, Leprechauns, Water spirits.

- Generally uninterested in human affairs
- Known for practical jokes without regard for consequences
- Love art and mimic human creativity

### Elves

Faerie race resembling slender humans.

- Pointed ears, large cat-like eyes, slender build
- Wise and spiritual
- Possess innate magic
- Expert archers and hunters
- Anashina's favored race

## Notable Creatures

### Dragons

**Origo** — ancient dragon. Obsessed with treasure and magic. Lives in the northwestern mountains. Highly dangerous and disrespectful to other creatures.

**Seven Wyverns** — minor dragons in the eastern mountains. Active threats to trolls and Araxi. Treasure collectors. Territorial.

**Aia** — female water dragon. Inhabits the Water Maze on the southern island. Lazy and prefers solitude. Less aggressive than other dragons.

### Phooka

Humanoid faeries with goat heads and pale-blue cold eyes. Lifespan ~300 years. Move 45.

- Regenerate 1 BP per round
- Iron weapons reduce regen to 1 BP per week
- Iron gives no armor protection against them
- Once per day, transform into a large eagle (carry adult human, 10 minutes)

### Treant

Tree-folk. Lifespan ~1000 years; mature at 100. Move 3.

- Indistinguishable from other trees when not moving (perfect camouflage when still)
- Some have magical powers — Anashina, Innate, or Natural Magick
- Size ranges 5O6+30

### Raven Demon

Indefinite lifespan. Move 80 (flying), 50 (walking). Size 6.

## Relations Between Peoples

### Alliances
- Humans + Dwarves — trade partners, mutual respect
- Elves + Faeries — natural kinship through Anashina

### Conflicts
- Dwarves vs Trolls — ancient hatred
- Dwarves vs Araxi — active hostility
- Humans vs Araxi — defensive conflicts
- Trolls vs Everyone — solitary aggression

### Neutral
- Faeries + Humans — minimal interaction, occasional pranks
- Dragons + most races — dragons generally ignore others unless provoked or treasure is involved

# Mythology

Source: [d6gaming.org/Mythology](https://d6gaming.org/index.php/Mythology)

## Creation

The universe began with **Uhr**, an all-knowing deity seeking entertainment through creation. Uhr generated **Magick**, representing chaos and mystery. Their interaction produced the foundational elements:

- **Alesia** (Earth)
- **Walmaer** (Water)
- **Shalissa** (Wind)
- **Ikalio** (Fire / Sun)

**Ielina** watches over the world while Ikalio rests, and is the timekeeper, managing the cycles of existence.

## Primary Gods (The Six)

The core pantheon is the four element-rulers plus Ielina (moon) and the Time Lords (preservers of existence).

| God | Sex | Domain |

- **Oddmar** — service

**Considered cursed**: Man Peggon, Elesi, Mestronorpha, Maleko.

## Religious Structure

Followers organize into cults per deity with ranked membership:

- **Lay Members** — no special benefits
- **Initiates** — gain a +3 bonus on their god's holy day
- **Priests** — gain +3 for five days before/after holy days; +6 on the day itself

Each god grants specific powers to their followers.

## Holy Days

One holy day per year per deity. Notable: every primary and secondary god has a named holy day; lesser-deity holy days fall on their day of the week within their patron month.
